Girls Generation (SNSD)

The Queen of K-Pop

Here's a timeline of Girls Generation

  • 18 July 2007 - Debut as a 9 members group under SM Entertainment
  • 11 Oct 2017 - First music show win
  • 2007 - Best Rookie in Golden-Disc Award and Seoul Music Award. Members to begin their personal activity after the first release of full album "Girls Generation"
  • 2009 - Published mega hit songs "Gee" and "Genie", with 3 successive albums to reach over 100 thousands sales volume. Hold the highest music boardcast ranking in Korea with "Music Center", the record has not been broken ever-since. Golden-Disk top song award (streaming) with "Gee". Began Asia concert tour.
  • 2010 - Keeping the sucessful strike and enormous popularity with albums "Oh" and "Run Devil Run". Japan debut with great success with golden disk certification in Japan with "Genie". Highets audience record in their Taiwan concert for non-Taiwanese female artist. Best Album of Golden-Disk Award with "Oh", the first female artist in Korea to be awarded with this grand price. "Daesang" (biggest award) in Seoul Music Award, as the first female artist to have two successive year to be awarded
  • 2011 - Top selling album in Korea with "The Boys", and with its repackage verison "Mr Taxi" with English version of "The Boys", they gained International popularity not within Asia, but far-reaching to the US, gaining appearence on shows like "Late Show with David Letterman" and "Live! with Kelly", as well as some mixing works with Snoop Dogg
  • 2012 - Sub-unit "TaeTiSeo" and keeping their popularity in Asia with "PAPARAZZI".
  • 2013 - Reached another spike in their career with album "I Got A Boy" with title song of the same name - a collaboration between European songwritting team "Dsign Music" and SM Entertainment. It gained various International attension with coverage from various source including "MTV", "BillBoard", "The Rocks Magazine", "Times Magazin" and "FUSE TV", making this comback of Girls Generation an International topic. 2nd Japan tour and first International tour. "Best MV" award in the first Youtube award ceremony
  • 2014 - Release of "Mr Mr". 3rd Japan tour. "TaeTiSeo" comeback. Jessica forced to leave the group. All remaining members renewed their contract with SM Entertainment. First Toyko Dome concert
  • 2015 - Comeback with "Party" as their pre-release song of the 5th full album. "Party" reached an ALLKILL on all music charts in Korea. 1st position in music charts in other Asian locations such as Hong Kong, Indonisia, Taiwan, Thailand, Singapore etc, and gained 7 music shows wins in total, followed by their offical comeback song "Lion Heart" and the realse of 5th full album, which gained the 1st position at BillBoard Internation chart. "Lion Heart" gained a total of 14 wins in music shows. Leader Taeyeon solo debuted with "I". Christmas comeback of "TaeTiSeo" with special album "Dear Santa". Members collaborated with SM song projects "SM Station"
  • 2016 - Tiffany solo debuted with mini album "I Just Wanna Dance".
  • 2017 - Seohyun solo debuted with "Don't Say No". Release of 10 year anniversary album "Holiday Night". Tiffany, Sooyoung and Seohyun did not renew their contract with SM, other members remain. Girls Generation will not disband dispite members will be in different agency
  • 2018 - Golden-Disk "Bonsang" award with "Holiday Night", once again proving their status in Korea as "National Girl Group". New sub-unit "Oh! GG". Member Yuri solo debuted with "The First Scene"
  • 2020 - The 5 members Taeyeon, Sunny, Hyoyun, Yuri and Yoona renewed contract with SM
  • 2022 - Hyoyun release her solo album "Deep". 15 Anniverary album "Forever 1" along with fan meeting.

... and the legend continues ...
